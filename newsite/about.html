Frequently Asked Questions

What levels do you support?

We support all levels from beginner (A1) to advanced (C1) according to the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages.

Do you offer business English?

Yes! We have specialized content for business communication, including emails, presentations, and meetings.

Can I track my progress?

Absolutely! Our platform provides detailed analytics and progress tracking to help you monitor your improvement.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, we offer a free trial so you can experience our platform before committing to a subscription.
